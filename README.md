# Biller 🚗📋

Biller is a simple, stress-free billing app and digital logbook built specifically for cab drivers, tour operators, and personal transport providers. 

Instead of dealing with messy paper notebooks or expensive tracking software, Biller helps you track your trips using your phone's real-time GPS location, log your daily drives, and create bills manually with total control.

---

## ✨ What Biller Does for You

*   **📍 Real-Time Location Tracking:** Uses your phone's GPS to see exactly where you are traveling during a trip. No expensive external hardware required.
*   **✍️ Flexible Manual Billing:** You have complete control. The app doesn't force a calculated fare on you—drivers can type in any final amount based on what was agreed with the customer, plus any extra parking, toll, or waiting charges.
*   **📖 Automatic Logbook:** Keeps an organized, permanent record of your trips, routes, dates, and times so you never lose track of your work history.
*   **🗺️ Built on Free Maps:** No hidden costs or expensive map subscription fees. The app uses high-quality, free mapping solutions to track routes.
*   **🚀 Seamless Driver Onboarding:** A smooth, step-by-step setup process designed to get drivers up, running, and creating bills within minutes.

---

## 📱 How It Works (Step-by-Step)

1.  **Quick Onboarding:** Open the app, set up your driver profile, and you are ready to roll.
2.  **Start a Trip:** Tap to start tracking. The app will record your route using your phone's live location.
3.  **Finish & Bill:** Once the trip ends, simply enter the final amount you want to charge. 
4.  **Save to Logbook:** The trip details, route, and your entered billing amount are instantly saved into your permanent digital history book.

---

## 🎯 Who is this for?

*   Independent cab drivers and taxi owners.
*   Tour and travel operators who need a reliable trip log.
*   Anyone managing a small fleet of vehicles who wants to keep records straight without expensive enterprise software.
